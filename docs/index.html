Detoxification

The sauna experience at Plunsana transcends traditional relaxation techniques by offering a profound detoxification process that engages the entire body. Through the mechanism of induced sweating, saunas serve as an effective means to expel toxins and impurities accumulated in the body. This natural purification process is facilitated by the elevated temperatures within the sauna, which stimulate the sweat glands and increase blood flow. As the body sweats, it not only sheds water but also releases a significant amount of these stored toxins, including heavy metals and chemicals absorbed from the environment.

A 4-Step Journey to Inner Peace and Vitality

1

Define Your Wellness Objectives

Goals are the starting point for any sauna therapy regimen. Whether you're looking to detoxify, relieve stress, improve cardiovascular health, or aid muscle recovery, clearly defining your objectives will help tailor your sauna sessions for maximum benefit.

2

Hydration

Hydration is essential before entering a sauna. Due to the high temperatures and the sweating process, it's crucial to drink water beforehand to ensure your body remains adequately hydrated throughout the session.

3

Gradually Increase Session Time

Start with shorter sessions of around 5-10 minutes and gradually increase the duration as your body becomes accustomed to the heat. This approach helps acclimate your body, reducing the risk of overheating.

4

Listen to Your Body and Cool Down

After exiting the sauna, allow your body to cool down gradually. Take a lukewarm shower, drink water to rehydrate, and rest for a few minutes to let your body temperature return to normal.
