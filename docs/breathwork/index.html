A 4-Step Journey to Inner Peace and Vitality

1. Awareness

Start by finding a quiet, comfortable space where you can sit or lie down without interruptions. Close your eyes and bring your attention to your natural breathing pattern. Notice the rhythm, depth, and sensations of each breath without trying to change anything.

2. Engage in Diaphragmatic Breathing

Place one hand on your abdomen and the other on your chest. As you inhale, focus on expanding your belly, feeling your hand rise, and minimizing the movement in your chest. This technique enhances oxygen exchange and activates the body's relaxation response.

3. Controlled Breathing

After mastering diaphragmatic breathing, experiment with varying the length of your inhales and exhales. A common method is the 4-7-8 technique: inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, and exhale for 8 seconds.

4. Practice Visualization

As you continue with controlled breathing, visualize a wave of relaxation sweeping through your body with each exhale, or imagine exhaling stress and inhaling calm. This step combines the power of the mind with the physiological benefits of Breathwork.
